definition:
P is point in triangle
A, B, C are triangle verts
P = A  + u(B - A) + v(C - A)


pseudocode:
triangleArea = areaOfTriangle(A, B, C)
u = areaOfTriangle(C, A, P) / triangleArea
v = areaOfTriangle(B, A, P) / triangleArea

fn areaOfTriangle(p1, p2, p3):
    p2.relativeTo(p1).magnitude() * p3.relativeTo(p1).magnitude() * sin(angle) / 2
